What is Join?

Join is a powerful Kanban project management tool that helps teams efficiently organize and manage their projects. With a user-friendly interface and a variety of features, Join provides an intuitive platform for collaboration and task progress.

How to use it

  1. 1.

    Create Kanban boards: Join allows users to create customizable Kanban boards to visualize their projects. Teams can divide their work into columns representing different stages or categories, such as "To Do," "In Progress," and "Completed." This provides a clear overview of the project and helps team members understand the workflow.

  2. 2.

    Capture tasks: Within each Kanban board, team members can capture tasks by creating cards. These cards represent individual tasks or work items. Users can add descriptions, attachments, due dates, and assignees to provide comprehensive information about each task. By capturing tasks in Join, teams have a centralized location to track and manage their work.

  3. 3.

    Delegate tasks: Join simplifies task delegation and collaboration. Team members can assign tasks to specific individuals, ensuring clarity and accountability. By assigning tasks, it becomes clear who is responsible for completing each task, reducing confusion and enabling efficient teamwork. Additionally, Join allows users to set deadlines and reminders, ensuring that everyone stays on track and meets project milestones.
